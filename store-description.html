Grand Chess

A chess variant that breaks free from the 8x8 grid. Play on boards from 3x3 up to a massive 99x99 squares, with multiple piece setup modes that create endless strategic possibilities.

Features

  • Variable board sizes - From tiny 3x3 puzzles to epic 99x99 battlefields
  • Multiple setup modes:
    • Two Lines - Classic setup with two ranks of pieces
    • One Line - Chess960-style randomized back rank
    • Three Lines - Dense formations for intense battles
    • Custom - Design your own starting positions with mirroring support
  • Play your way - Local Player vs Player, challenge the AI, or play online
  • FEN support - Import and export positions to share with others
  • Intuitive controls - Pan, zoom, and navigate large boards with ease

Online Multiplayer

Play against friends over the network using direct IP connection.

Privacy Note: Online play requires sharing your IP address. Only share your IP with people you know and trust. Your IP address can reveal your approximate location and should be treated as personal information.
